$f^*(x^*) := \sup_{x \in I} (x^*x - f(x))$ for $x^* \in I^*, x \in I$
### ✍️ Notation
- $I \subset \mathbb{R}$ is an [[interval]] in the [[real line]]
- $f : I \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ is a [[convex function]]
- $I^* := \{ x^* \in \mathbb{R} : f^*(x^*) < \infty \}$
- $\sup$ is the [[supremum operator]]
