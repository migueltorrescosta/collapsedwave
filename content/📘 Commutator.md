$[A, B] := AB - BA$

The commutator is:
1. [[anti-commutative]] : $[A, B] = - [B, A]$
2. [[bilinear]] : $[xA + yB, C] = x[A, C] + y[B,C]$
3. $[A, BC] = [A,B]C + B[A,C]$
4. follows the [[Jacobi identity]]: $[A, [B,C]] + [B, [C,A]] + [C, [A,B]] = 0$

>[!info]-
> $x,y \in \mathbb{C}$
