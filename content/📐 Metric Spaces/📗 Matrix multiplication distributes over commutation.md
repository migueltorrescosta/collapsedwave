$[A, BC] = [A,B]C + B[A,C]$

# Proof
$$
\begin{array}{rll}
[A,BC] & = ABC - BCA & \text{Adding "zero"} \\
& = ABC - BAC + BAC - BCA & \text{Rearranging the expression} \\
& = (AB - BA)C + B(AC-CA) & \text{Applying the definition of commutator}\\
& = [A,B]C + B[A,C]
\end{array}
$$
