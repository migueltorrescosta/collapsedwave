$$
\left \{
\begin{matrix}
\frac{\partial}{\partial q_i}H = -\dot p_i \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial p_i}H = \dot q_i \\
\end{matrix}
\right .
$$

Where:
- $H$ is the [[classical hamiltonian]]
- $q_i$ is the [[position]] of the $i$th particle.
- $p_i$ is the [[momentum]] of the $i$th particle
