$\braket{A}_\psi  := \braket{\psi, A \psi}$
### ✍️ Notation
- $\braket{ \cdot, \cdot }: H \times H \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ is an [[Inner Product]] on $H$
- $A: H \rightarrow H$ is a [[📘 Self Adjoint Operator]]
- $\psi \in H$ is a [[quantum state vector]]
