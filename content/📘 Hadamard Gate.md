A [[Quantum Gate]] acting on a single [[qubit]], applying the transformations

$$\ket{0} \rightarrow \frac{\ket{0} + \ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}}$$
$$\ket{1} \rightarrow \frac{\ket{0} - \ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}}$$
